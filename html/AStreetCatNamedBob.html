	    Name: A Street Cat Named Bob;
		Author: James Bowen;
		Published In: 2014;
		Number Of pages: 248;
		Book Description: The book tells the story of James Bowed a street musician who still struggles with his drug addiction and is on his way to completely giving
		up on the substances. One night, after coming home from a not so good day on the streets he found an injured tomcat  curled up in the hallway of his apartment building,
	    and from that day, his life took an unexpected turn for both of them.
		
		    Failing to find the cat's owner James takes him in and names him Bob despite the fact that he could barely feed himself, yet that did bot stop him from doing 
		 everything in his power to help his new best friend.Bob quickly takes a liking to James and the two of them form a bond stronger than anything.
		    The two of them go through many hardships together and each one of them makes the pair realize just how important they are for each other. Together they move from
		singing on the streets of London to selling newspapers and being a true attraction for the numerous tourists that were going to London.
		
          "    There's a famous quote I read somewhere. It says we are all given second chances every day of our lives. They are there for the taking, it's just 
		   that we don't usually take them. 
		       I spend a big chunk of my life proving that quote. I was given a lot of opportunities, sometimes on a daily basis.For a long time I failed to take  
		   any of them, but then, in the early spring of 2007, that finally began to change. It was then that I befriended Bob. Looking back on it, something tells 
		   me it may have been his second chance too.	"
