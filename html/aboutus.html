Welcome and thank you for choosing our website. We are a website dedicated to books and their authors. On our website, users are welcome to rate books, to provide additional information about them and the authors, to request books and to rate also the film-adaptation of the books. We support free speech as long as it is purely related to the topic of the book, the author or the film. Harmful language towards other users will not be tolerated.
